Strong passwords are one of the most important parts of staying safe online. Hackers often rely on simple guessing techniques or leaked databases. If your password is weak, reused, or easy to guess, your information is at risk.

Saving passwords in browsers can be risky.

Avoid simple or common passwords.

Never share passwords insecurely.

Always enable multi-factor authentication.

Don't write passwords in unsafe places.

Use unique passwords for every account.
