Content

Home page

Strong passwords are one of the most important parts of staying safe online. Hackers often rely on really simple guessing techniques or leaked password databases to gain access to accounts. If your password is weak, Reaused, or easy to guess, it puts your information at serious risk.

A password should be longe, unique, and difficut to guess. This means avoiding common words, and poersonal information. Instead you should use a mix of uppercase letters, lowercase letters, and special characters.

Password Checker page

As you type a password, the tool will analyze it based on length, complexity, and unpredicatbility. This tool is completely local. No input is stored, saved, or trasnmitted.
