Home Checker

As you type a password, this tool analyzes its strength based on length, complexity, and unpredictability. This tool runs completely locally. Nothing you type is stored or sent anywhere.

Strength:

Strong passwords should be long, unique, and include a mix of uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols. Avoid using personal information or common words.


It is also a good idea to use different passwords for each account. It should not include any personal information like your birthday or address because identity theft and data breaches can compromise this information. Its also a good idea to not use personal information that can be accessed by anyone.


A password should also not contain consecutive letters or numbers (i.e. ABC or 123) because these are easy to guess.
